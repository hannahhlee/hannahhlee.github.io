Create the Sauce

  1. Rough chop the garlic, ginger and shallots.


  2. Add them into a blender or food processor and create a coarse paste. Remove from blender and set aside.


  3. Add the Canned Tomato to the blender or food processor and create a coarse paste. Remove from blender and strain. Retain liquid.


  4. Heat a pan and melt the Clarified Butter.


  5. Add Whole Cinnamon, Clove, Black Cardamom, Coriander Seed, Cumin Seed, Mustard Seed and Bay Leaf to Clarifiied Butter.


  6. Heat spices in oil until the mustard seed begins to sizzle.


  7. Add curry paste to oil and fry under low heat until ingrededients begin to caramelize.


  8. Add Chopped tomato and fry under low heat until tomatoes begin to dry.


  9. Add the Cashews, Turmeric Powder, and Chilli Powder and fry until fragrant.


  10. Add Tomato Juice and simmer until juice no longer tastes raw.


  11. Remove ingredients from pan and carefully place inside blender.


  12. Blend until smooth, thinning the paste with coconut milk to a sauce like consistency.


  13. Strain through sieve and discard the grit.


  14. Add salt and sugar to taste.


Using the Sauce

  1. Brown meat and add sauce. Simmer until tender.


  2. To finish sauce, season with Garam Masala and Kasoori Methi. Fold in Yogurt.


  3. Garnish with chopped cilantro, golden raisins, and Thai Curry Cashews.
